Discussion

This article discusses that tax is the intersection of all participants in the market or society, also the unity of distribution tool and value measure. So, it is needful and feasible to establish a system:

Symmetric Value and Credit Talent named Tax/Fin. Trone (Chains) which weighs/measures the value or credit by the following functional equation:

T(x,y)*= Lg x(tax)* + x(tax)/y(relative value of tax) (%) … errata( erring stain record )

Where T denotes tax, x denotes the tax category and amount, y denotes the relative value of tax (or the comparable data series with tax, such as comparable goods, logistics and invoice, sales, cost, income, etc.), errata denotesthe erring stain record.

That is to say, we establish a value and credit system through a counteracting institution which balances financial accountingdata or other factors by tax, assesses and adjuststhe participant’s condition (includingfinance,account,wealth, credit,quality，rights，duties, etc.) matching with taxation, further distinguishes good from evil，enhances goodness and chastise evil, which aims to mitigate information asymmetry, and leads a healthy value orientation, then improve upon the market and society.

Nowadays, lots ofdata or indexes are easy to manipulate at very low cost, while hard and expensive for users to verify.

现行诸多数据或指标，容易造假，且造假成本低，但检测的难度和成本大。

Taxation is the state machine based on the value of public goods. It is the distribution of wealth, and the intersection that relates to all market participants, including supply, capital, manufacture, service, purchase，etc. Further, it reflects and adjuststhe condition of market participants. As thus, building on the mathematical function(tax, financial information, etc.), we can establish a system: Symmetric Value and Credit Talent, named Tax/Fin. Tronefor market participants.

税赋是基于公用品价值的国家机器，是对财富的分配，是市场各方——包括供应、资本、生产、服务、购买等各方关系的交集，进而反映并调控市场诸方状况。因此，基于函数(税收,财务信息等)，我们可以建立一套服务于市场各方的系统：对称性价值与资信天赋——称为“赋权称链”。

The system aims to lead value orientation and value appreciation, improve transparency and self-discipline and heteronomy, alleviate the information asymmetry, control risk and prevent crisis, promote fairness, and stimulate healthy market and social development.

Market Failure


Markets with asymmetric information：participants on one side of the market have much better information than those on the other. And that, self-interested market behavior does not always act fairly orproduce felicitous social consequences.

This creates an imbalance of power in transactions among different participants, which can sometimes cause the transactions to go awry, a kind of market failure in the worst case.Examples of this problem areunscrupulous spoof，adverse selection, moral hazard, or information monopoly.

Even worse, it may cause economic and social crisis.

Synopsis of The theory

Tax and Price(税收与价格)

Tax is a distribution of wealth.

It is also a price that citizens pay for public goods.( Refer to: Lindahl prices, also called Lindahl taxes, proposed by Swedish Economist Erik Robert Lindahl in 1919 .)

From the perspective of taxation system and other rules,we can derive the following formula:

P(total price of goods)=P0(price of goods without tax) + Tax

Tax=P(price of goods) * Tax Rate

Tax value and information 税收价值与信息论

  • 1.

    V(total value of goods)

    =V0(1-5): goods value without tax – relative value of tax, representing relevantrights and duties

    +Vt: public goods value – absolute value of tax, representing public rights and duties

    +V△:appreciation value– combination leading to value increase/decrease or surplus value

    +Vi:information value–combination leading to information value

  • 2.

    Vt=F(V), V=F'(Vt)

    (a mathematic function and inverse function between Vt and V）

  • 3.

    Taxation is the intersection of all participants in the market and society, and a combination of economy leverage, value scale (card), information tool, monitor, adjustment, control, and stabilizer.

That is to say: In the market economics, taxation is the intersection of all market participants. It is a distributionof goods, a price that citizens pay or return for public goods. The price also reflects therelevant value in a measure. We develop an anatomy of finance and value, confronting finance with the relevanttax, distinguishing the different parts of value for market participants as V0(1-5): goods value without tax – relative value of tax; Vt: public goods value – absolute value of tax; V△:appreciation value of combination; Vi: information value of combination, etc.

Further, taxation is a combination of economic leverage, self-stabilizer, value measure, information tool, monitor and adjustment. To some degree, it reflects and adjusts the condition of market.

Mechanism design

ISOTAF , Symmetric Value and Credit Talent: Tax/Fin. Trone.


Based on the contents of the paragraph above，we can see that it is needful and feasible to establishes a symmetric Value and Credit Talent for market participants with function (tax, fin.). Let us describe this system of the form T(x,y)* (or called Value-Credit Weight index, T* for short ).

Resulting from the tax law and financial regulation, government taxation levy, tax assessment and actual payment, this system gains highly valuable credibility on good authority.

Talent 财赋资信的衡量

T(x) * G(y) = Vt ( absolute value of tax ) + V ( relative value of tax ) … Errata [ erring stain record ]

= Lgx[ tax(thousand) ] * G(y)[ x(tax) / y ( relative value of tax ) (%) … Errata [ erring credit ]

Where T denotes tax, and X denotes the tax category and amount [ such as revenue (turnover) tax, property and resource tax, income tax, social security tax, other tax, whole tax ].

y denotes the relative value of tax [ the comparable data series with taxation such as logistics and fund flow, information flow, invoice chain, internet of things，etc. Or the relative financial accounting information with x(tax) such as comparable assets, capital, equity, interest, liabilities, turnover or revenue, value, business, cost, energy source, goods, income, materials, person, property and resource, technology, whole factors, etc. ]

* (asterisk) denotes star level. G denotes grade, and G(y) denotes the grade of x/y (%).

Errata [erring credit] denotes the index of Special Tax Adjustments [ Lgtax-adjustment(thousand) ] concerning vicious finance such as duty evasion, fraud, bribe，penalty，and other unscrupulous income or expense.

Score 财赋资信的计算

For example:

In thousands of USD (K for short), assume that Factory A realized A goods revenue (turnover) 800 during a period , and paid or assumed tax 100 [revenue (turnover) tax 50 , social security tax 10, income tax 20, other tax 20]. Other than that, the Special Tax Adjustments concerning vicious finance is 10 (Note: the victim needs to reduce the tax payment for the adjustment). Then:

T(x)*G(y)= Lg[x(tax)] * x(tax)/y [relative ](%)…E[ erring credit ]

T(x)*G(tr)= Lg[x(tax)(K)] * x(tax)/tr[relative revenue or turnover](%) … E[ erring credit ]

=Lg100(K) *100/800(tr)(%) …E: Lg[ (Special Tax Adjustments)10(K)]

= Lg102(K) * 12.5(tr) (%) … E: Lg101(K)

=2*(K)12.5(tr) … E 1(*)

Read as：2Star, 12.5(tr)rating…Erring credit: 1 BlackStar.

Also because of the symmetric relationship between tax and certain proportions of finance (or other factors), when financial manipulation or adverse circumstancesoccurs, the system can reveal or control it symmetricallyin a way.

For example, in the case above, assume that because of financial manipulation or other reason, the turnover figure becomes $8000K from $800K and almost nothing else changes. Well then, the relative valueof G(tr)declines instead of increasing from 12.5(r)(%) to 1.25(r)(%).

∵G(tr)= x(tax)/tr(revenue or turnover)(%)

∴(Normal) G(tr)= 100/800=12.5 (%).

(Particular)G’(tr)= 100/8000=1.25 (%).

Deviationrate=(Normal) G(tr) / (Particular) G’(tr) (%)=12.5 /1.25(%)=1000 (%).

It reflects obviously that Factory A may have serious problemssuch as manipulation and fraud, or operating condition, or product quality, etc.
